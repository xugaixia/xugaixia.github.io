Biography

Gaixia Xu received her Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering in 2005 from Zhejiang University. From 2005 to 2007, she completed a postdoctoral fellowship in the Institute of Optoelectronics, Shenzhen University. From 2007 to 2008, she completed a postdoctoral fellowship in the Institute of Laser, Photonics and Biophotonics, Optoelectronics, State University of New York. She has joined Shenzhen University since 2008 and been appointed full professor since 2013. Her current research interests include biomedical applications of multi-mode nanomaterials (CdSe, CdTe, InP, CuS, AIE, Black phosphorus) and novel optical imaging methods.

I am looking for self-motivated Postdoc/PhD/RA/Interns, who are interested in biophotonics, nanomedicine, AIE, and bioinformatics. Welcome to drop me an email with your CV and transcripts. Remote collaboration is also welcome!

If you are an SZU student interested in doing research with me, please send me an email.

Due to the large amount of emails I receive, I may not be able to respond to each one individually.
